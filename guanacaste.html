    • Inshore Fishing (Half Day, 4 hours): Ideal for those looking to catch various types of fish with a high success rate.     • Inshore Fishing (3/4 Day, 6 hours or Full Day, 8 hours): For anglers wanting to target a wider variety of fish, this trip extends to 20 miles offshore.     • Offshore Deep Sea Fishing (8 hours): Perfect for those seeking big-game fish like Marlin, Sailfish, and Bull Mahi-Mahi. This adventure takes you 35-40 miles offshore on a larger boat due to rougher seas. The private tour, for 1-4 people, starts at $1100 and can go up to $3000, depending on the boat size. The trip includes lunch, drinks, beer, fruit, snacks, and all fishing equipment.
