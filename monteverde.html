  • Santa Elena Reserve Hike

    Explore the Santa Elena Cloud Forest Reserve in Monteverde, Costa Rica, and immerse yourself in the beauty of one of the region’s most diverse ecosystems. Known for its abundant epiphytes, such as orchids, bromeliads, and moss-covered trees, the reserve thrives in its humid environment. These unique plants store water during the dry season, making them crucial to the area’s ecosystem.
    Managed by the Colegio Técnico Profesional de Santa Elena, this reserve not only protects local wildlife but also supports community development through its funding. The tour begins with a hotel pick-up around 8:30 AM, followed by a 2.5-hour guided hike along the 3.4 km El Encantado trail. As you walk through the cloud forest, you’ll experience breathtaking views, spot native birds and mammals, and enjoy the beauty of the lush landscape. The tour also offers stunning views of the Arenal Volcano in the distance.
    Ideal for nature lovers and birdwatchers, this tour provides an unforgettable experience in one of Costa Rica’s most biodiverse environments.
