  • Night Hiking Arenal

    Explore Arenal’s unique wild frog sanctuary during its most active hours, the night, when colorful tree frogs and other rainforest wildlife emerge. Home to over 28 species of frogs, the sanctuary is nestled within Arenal Oasis, a private wildlife reserve. This reserve features fresh spring-fed ponds, which are carefully maintained by the Rojas Bonilla family. Their dedication to preserving native Arenal plants creates the perfect habitat for frogs, connecting the rainforests of Arenal Volcano and El Río Burio.
    The night tour takes you through the sanctuary’s tranquil trails, allowing you to observe the vibrant wildlife of the rainforest under the guidance of trained naturalists. As you walk along the flat trails, you’ll learn about the various species living in the area, with your guide pointing out hidden creatures in the dense rainforest.
